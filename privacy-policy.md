# Privacy Policy for StageSet

Effective date: 8 April 2026

StageSet is an offline-first app for creating and managing songs, chords, and set lists.

At this time, StageSet:

- does not require an account
- does not send your data to the developer
- does not use advertising
- does not use analytics or tracking
- stores your songs and set lists locally on your device

Any content you create in StageSet remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it yourself.

If StageSet adds cloud sync, accounts, analytics, or other online features in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.
